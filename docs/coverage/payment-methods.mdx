<p class="intro-subheading">
  Brale enables offchain deposits and withdraws into and out of U.S. bank accounts using supported transfer types.
</p>

## United States 

Supported USD transfer types include:

- `wire`
- `ach-credit`
- `ach-debit`
- `same-day-ach-credit`
- `rtp-credit`
