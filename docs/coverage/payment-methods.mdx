<p class="intro-subheading">
  Brale enables deposits and withdraws into and out of U.S. bank accounts.
</p>

## United States

Supported methods include:

- Wire (Domestic only)
- ACH

Additional payment methods including international wires and same-day ACH will be supported in Q3 2025.
